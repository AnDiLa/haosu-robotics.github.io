Shoulder Exoskeleton

We created our most lightweight portable shoulder exoskeleton that provide high torque assistance and are suitable for for heterogeneous users with different levels of impairments.

Hardware Features

Our shoulder exoskeleton assists 2 DoF movements (flexion/extension, abduction/adduction) while most robots are either passive or only provide 1 DOF assistance;

Thanks to our high torque density motor and compact electronics design, our shoulder exoskeleton is fully portable and modular and can be used for bilateral or unilateral assistance (e.g., stroke is hemiparesis and requires unilateral assistance);
